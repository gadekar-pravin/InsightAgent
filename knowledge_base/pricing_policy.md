# TechCorp Pricing Policy

## Current Pricing Structure (Effective November 1, 2024)

### Price Increase Announcement
**Effective Date**: November 1, 2024
**Increase**: 12% across all product lines

#### Rationale
- Increased infrastructure costs (+18% YoY)
- Enhanced feature set and capabilities
- Market positioning alignment
- Investment in customer support expansion

### Impact Analysis

#### Revenue Impact
- Expected incremental revenue: $1.2M in Q4 2024
- Full-year 2025 impact: $4.8M additional revenue

#### Customer Impact
- Enterprise customers: Generally accepting due to value delivered
- SMB customers: Some pushback expected, mitigation plans in place
- Consumer segment: Highest churn risk from price increase

### West Region Legacy Pricing Exception

**Important**: A significant portion of West region customers remain on legacy pricing agreements.

#### Legacy Pricing Details
- **Affected Customers**: 340 accounts (representing $2.1M ARR)
- **Contract Terms**: 3-year agreements signed in 2022
- **Price Lock**: Original 2022 pricing locked until contract renewal
- **Average Discount vs Current**: 28% below current pricing

#### Renewal Timeline
- Q1 2025: 45 accounts ($280K ARR) up for renewal
- Q2 2025: 120 accounts ($850K ARR) up for renewal
- Q3 2025: 175 accounts ($970K ARR) up for renewal

#### Impact on West Region Performance
The legacy pricing concentration in the West region means:
1. Lower revenue per customer compared to other regions
2. Reduced ability to offset competitive pressure through pricing
3. Renewal periods represent both risk (churn) and opportunity (price normalization)
