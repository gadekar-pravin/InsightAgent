# Customer Segmentation Guide

## Segment Definitions

TechCorp classifies customers into three primary segments based on company size, revenue potential, and service requirements.

### Enterprise Segment

#### Definition
- **Company Size**: 500+ employees
- **Annual Contract Value (ACV)**: $50,000+
- **Decision Cycle**: 3-6 months
- **Key Stakeholders**: C-suite, IT Director, Procurement

#### Characteristics
- Require dedicated account management
- Custom implementation and integration needs
- High service level expectations (99.9% uptime SLA)
- Multi-year contract preference
- Security and compliance requirements (SOC 2, HIPAA, etc.)

#### Lifetime Value Benchmarks
- **Average LTV**: $85,000
- **Top Quartile LTV**: $120,000+
- **Bottom Quartile LTV**: $45,000

#### Churn Profile
- **Average Churn Rate**: 2.1% annually
- **Primary Churn Reasons**:
  1. M&A activity (35%)
  2. Competitive displacement (25%)
  3. Budget cuts (20%)
  4. Product fit issues (20%)

### SMB Segment (Small & Medium Business)

#### Definition
- **Company Size**: 20-499 employees
- **Annual Contract Value (ACV)**: $5,000 - $49,999
- **Decision Cycle**: 2-8 weeks
- **Key Stakeholders**: Business Owner, Department Head, IT Manager

#### Characteristics
- Self-service oriented with optional support
- Standard implementation packages
- Monthly or annual billing preference
- Price-sensitive but value-focused
- Growing need for scalability

#### Lifetime Value Benchmarks
- **Average LTV**: $18,000
- **Top Quartile LTV**: $25,000+
- **Bottom Quartile LTV**: $8,000

#### Churn Profile
- **Average Churn Rate**: 4.8% annually
- **Primary Churn Reasons**:
  1. Price sensitivity (40%)
  2. Business closure/downsizing (25%)
  3. Outgrown product (20%)
  4. Competitive switch (15%)

### Consumer Segment

#### Definition
- **User Type**: Individual users or very small teams (<20)
- **Annual Contract Value (ACV)**: Under $5,000
- **Decision Cycle**: Immediate to 1 week
- **Key Stakeholder**: Individual user

#### Characteristics
- Fully self-service
- Freemium model with upgrade path
- High volume, low touch
- Mobile-first expectations
- Social proof driven decisions

#### Lifetime Value Benchmarks
- **Average LTV**: $850
- **Top Quartile LTV**: $2,000+
- **Bottom Quartile LTV**: $500

#### Churn Profile
- **Average Churn Rate**: 8.5% annually
- **Primary Churn Reasons**:
  1. Found free alternative (35%)
  2. No longer needed (30%)
  3. Price increase sensitivity (20%)
  4. Feature gaps (15%)

## Upsell and Cross-sell Opportunities

### Enterprise → Enterprise+
- Advanced analytics add-on: $15K/year
- Premium support package: $25K/year
- Custom integrations: $50K+ one-time

### SMB → Enterprise
- Trigger: Company reaches 400+ employees
- Average deal size increase: 3.5x
- Conversion rate: 12% annually

### Consumer → SMB
- Trigger: Team collaboration features needed
- Average deal size increase: 8x
- Conversion rate: 3% annually
