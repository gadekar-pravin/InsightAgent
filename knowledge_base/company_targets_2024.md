## Performance Thresholds

### Underperformance Classification
- **Minor**: 5-10% below target - Manager review required
- **Moderate**: 10-20% below target - Director review and action plan required
- **Significant**: >20% below target - Executive intervention, immediate remediation plan

### Regional Accountability
Each regional VP is accountable for their target. Monthly progress reviews are conducted with the following escalation:
- Week 4 of month: If tracking >5% below monthly target, alert regional VP
- Week 8 of quarter: If tracking >10% below quarterly target, escalate to SVP Sales
- Week 12 of quarter: Final assessment and variance analysis due within 5 business days
